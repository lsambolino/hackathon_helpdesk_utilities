Predictive layer

Volume forecasts and root-cause clustering. Executive dashboard. First quarterly defects report.

Risks & guardrails

Regulatory

Human-in-the-loop on every external action. Full audit log. Models hosted in EU.

Hallucinations

Bot acts only on data retrieved from your systems (RAG). Actions go through workflow engine, not free text.

Workforce

Co-pilot before replacement. Operators use it, customers don't see it. FTE redeployed, not laid off.

Lock-in

Knowledge base remains your asset. Open standards. Models are interchangeable.
